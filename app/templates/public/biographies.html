(Daniela, traducir al español) In this section you will find biographies of Chilean performers and ensembles of art music during the 20th century, that are the product of archival research and interviews with performers and their families. The biographies were developed in the framework of the research project Fondo de la Música 583750 “Biographies of Chilean performers of art music, 1945–1995” (2021/22). Aside from the research team, several other researchers have contributed biographies. The authors’ names are indicated in each article. Did you not find what you were looking for? Do you think other performers or ensembles should be included in this web site? Would you like to collaborate with us? Don’t hesitate to write us at the following email: dfugellie@uahurtado.cl.
